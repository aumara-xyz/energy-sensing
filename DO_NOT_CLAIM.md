# Do Not Claim Ledger

This repository does not claim:

- proof that one pathway explains all chronic disease,
- proof that GLP-1R agonists are universal disease modifiers,
- proof that neurologic disease benefits follow from peripheral benefits,
- proof that AMPK or mTOR should be pushed in one fixed direction indefinitely,
- proof that topology or chirality observations are already clinically actionable,
- proof that GHP validates medical biology,
- proof that this document is treatment guidance,
- proof that any off-label combination should be used in patients.

## Safe Language

Use:

- "suggests"
- "motivates"
- "research program"
- "control-state model"
- "candidate biomarker"
- "stage-dependent"
- "target-engagement dependent"
- "falsifiable"

Avoid:

- "proves"
- "cures"
- "confirms"
- "works across all chronic disease"
- "clinical recommendation"

unless a claim is directly supported at that level.
